Pasta/Pizza Sauce

This pasta sauce works as a pizza sauce too! It's quick and easy to make and can be doubled or tripled. Substitute other cheeses and herbs for variety.

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Total Time: 20 mins Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons red wine
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil/li>
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and sauté until golden and fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Stir in tomato paste, water, Parmesan cheese, wine, sugar, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 10 minutes.
